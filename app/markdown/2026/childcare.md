<h2 id="signup">Sign Up</h2>


We’re offering subsidized, professional childcare at CascadiaJS so you can actually attend, learn, and connect. Childcare will be provided by the wonderful people at A-List Nanny Services. 

The cost is $100 for 1 child, and $25 for an additional child.  

<div class="cta secondary"><a href="/2026/tickets">Sign up</a></div>

<h2 id="hours">Hours</h2>

**June 1 & 2**<br/>
8:45 am-12:40 pm <br/>
1:40 pm-5:30 pm 
  
You're welcome to drop off and pick up your child at any time during these hours. We ask that you take your child during lunch to allow our caregivers a break. 

<h2 id="location">Location</h2>

Childcare will be located in Town Hall in the Green Room on the Forum level. 

<h2 id="bring">What to Bring</h2>

Please make sure everything is labeled with your child's name.

* Water bottle or cup
* Favorite toys, games and books (optional)

For babies and toddlers:

* portable crib or mat for naps
* breast milk/formula and bottles (in a cooler or insulated bag if it needs to be kept cool)
* diapers, wipes and a spare set of clothes

<h2 id="meals">Snacks & Meals</h2>
 
We'll provide some nut-free snacks and fruit. Please bring breakfast, lunch and any other nut-free snacks that your child enjoys.
