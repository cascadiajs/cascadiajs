Breakfast, lunch and dinner are available to attendees who have a green, Premium ticket. Coffee, tea and water stations are for everyone. 

[d] = contains dairy, [e] = contains egg, [g] = contains gluten, [gf] = gluten free, [n] = contains nuts, [p] = contains pork, [s] = contains seafood, [v] = vegan
